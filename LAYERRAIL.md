# LayerRail

LayerRail is a grant-ready cloud console and research PaaS/IaaS direction built on top of the Ubicloud open source infrastructure foundation.

The product narrative is:

- open cloud infrastructure for developers and researchers
- workspaces with budget rails and sponsored compute controls
- deployment passports for runtime, region, owner, and resource evidence
- research evidence exports for grant reporting and reproducibility

## Upstream Foundation

This fork is based on Ubicloud and keeps the upstream AGPL-3.0 license. Keep the original license and notices intact, and publish source for network-accessible modifications as required by AGPL-3.0.

Recommended remote setup:

```text
origin   https://github.com/mayowaoladosu/ubicloud.git
upstream https://github.com/ubicloud/ubicloud.git
```

Use `origin` for LayerRail work and `upstream` to pull Ubicloud updates.
